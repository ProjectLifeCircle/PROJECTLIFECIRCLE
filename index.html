We are on the Front Lines of California Wildfires

For more than 65 years, Project LIFE has been on the front lines of the world's greatest health challenges. We are a non-profit organization that is dedicated to helping communities affected by wildfires.

Help Us Rebuild Lives

About Project Life

Project Life is a non-profit organization focused on rebuilding communities after disasters, providing essential resources for the homeless, and bringing hope to those in need. We stand strong in the face of adversity, offering shelter, medical aid, and emotional support to those affected by the recent wildfires in Los Angeles.
